NovaTech AI — Company Knowledge Base
======================================

About NovaTech AI
-----------------
NovaTech AI is a Karachi-based artificial intelligence startup founded in 2021 by Fatima Al-Rashid
and Bilal Mahmood. The company specializes in building enterprise-grade AI solutions for logistics,
supply chain optimization, and intelligent document processing.

As of 2024, NovaTech employs over 120 engineers across offices in Karachi, Dubai, and London.
The company raised a $47 million Series B funding round in March 2024, led by Sequoia Capital.

Core Products
-------------
1. LogiSense Platform
   LogiSense is NovaTech's flagship product — an AI-powered logistics optimization platform.
   It uses real-time data from IoT sensors, weather APIs, and traffic systems to recommend
   optimal delivery routes. Clients report an average 23% reduction in delivery costs after
   deployment. LogiSense currently processes over 2 million delivery events per day.

2. DocuMind
   DocuMind is an intelligent document processing tool that uses large language models to
   extract, classify, and summarize information from invoices, contracts, and shipping documents.
   It supports 14 languages and achieves 97.3% extraction accuracy on structured documents.
   DocuMind integrates with SAP, Oracle, and Microsoft Dynamics out of the box.

3. SupplyIQ
   SupplyIQ is a supply chain intelligence dashboard that provides predictive analytics for
   inventory management, demand forecasting, and supplier risk assessment. It uses gradient
   boosting models trained on 5 years of historical data from partner companies.

Technology Stack
----------------
NovaTech's core ML infrastructure is built on PyTorch and deployed on AWS using Kubernetes.
The company uses a microservices architecture with gRPC for inter-service communication.
All models are versioned using MLflow and served through custom FastAPI endpoints.
Data pipelines are built on Apache Kafka for real-time streaming and Apache Airflow for batch.

Security & Compliance
---------------------
NovaTech is ISO 27001 certified and GDPR compliant. All customer data is encrypted at rest
using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. The company undergoes quarterly penetration
testing by a third-party security firm. PII data is anonymized at ingestion using a custom
tokenization pipeline before it enters any ML training loop.

Support & SLA

Standard tier customers receive 99.5% uptime SLA with business-hours support.
